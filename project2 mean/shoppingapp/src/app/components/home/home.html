Welcome to ShoppingAPI

This is a simple shopping application built with Angular, Node.js, and MongoDB. You can manage products easily by adding, updating, deleting, and viewing them in one place.

Use the navigation bar above to explore different features:

  • Products – View all available products
  • Add Product – Add new products to the system
  • Update Product – Edit existing product details
  • Delete Product – Remove products you no longer need

Start exploring and manage your shopping data with ease!
